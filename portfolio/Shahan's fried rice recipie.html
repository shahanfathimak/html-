Shahan's fried rice recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, day-old rice works best)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, chopped (you can use any color)
  • 1/2 cup peas (frozen peas work fine)
  • 1/2 cup carrots, chopped into small pieces
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional for extra flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (optional for an aromatic touch)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder or 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional for added protein)
  • Green onions (scallions), chopped for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Make sure the rice is cooked and cold. If you are using fresh rice, spread it on a tray to cool down before using.
    • Chop the vegetables and protein, if using.
  2. Cook the Eggs:
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Pour in the beaten eggs, scrambling them until fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry the Vegetables:
    • In the same pan, add the remaining oil.
    • Add the onions and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until soft and translucent.
    • Add the bell peppers, carrots, peas, and any other veggies you're using. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Add the Rice:
    • Add the cold rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and stir to mix it evenly with the vegetables.
  5. Flavor the Rice:
    • Add soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), garlic powder (or fresh garlic), and sesame oil. Stir everything well to coat the rice evenly with the sauces and flavors.
    • If using ginger, add it now for a fresh, zesty flavor.
  6. Add the Protein:
    • Stir in your choice of protein (chicken, shrimp, or tofu). Let it heat through, mixing it well with the rice and vegetables.
  7. Finish with Eggs:
    • Add the cooked scrambled eggs back into the pan, breaking them into smaller pieces. Stir everything to combine.
  8. Garnish:
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Garnish with fresh green onions (scallions) before serving.
  9. Serve:
    • Serve the fried rice hot, as a side or a main dish. Enjoy!
