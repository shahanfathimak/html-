Paradoxical Dreams

Shahan Fathima k

Deep in my sleep
In the world of dreams
I am in a Paradise
I glimpse a hill
Stride to reach the peak
Arrived the top
I could see the cloud close
Glance to down
Suddenly I lost my visibility
I slip off and drop to the bottom
Speechless breathless physique
Close the eyes to the darkness
And open the eyes to the real world
